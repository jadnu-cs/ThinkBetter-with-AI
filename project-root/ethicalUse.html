Ethical Use of Artificial intelligence

-The use of AI in academe must be accompanied with integrity and deep understanding of the knowledge provided. One must be able to assess, verify and integrate AI outputs in their own academic work. AI should be responsibly used as a tool that enhances learning.
To avoid plagiarism and misuse, you must properly cite the AI tools or the information used in the output. You can also expand the AI-generated ideas with your own words to avoid plagiarism.
AI tools could possibly generate inaccurate or outdated information. As a user it is essential to fact check the information given out by AI by finding other credible sources such as textbooks, research and many more.
AI tools can give out biased information due to how they are trained and what environment they are built in. Thus, leading to unfair and skewed information. It is also important to consider multiple perspectives when using AI.
Users should use AI primarily as a tool used to support learning and not replace the critical and creativity aspect of an individual. It is important to understand the limitations and ethical concepts when using AI. Users must show integrity, honesty and comprehension when implementing AI in their own work.
